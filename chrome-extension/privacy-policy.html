Market X-Ray Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Market X-Ray ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension ("Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

We do not collect any personal information that identifies you directly. The Extension operates locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The Extension may collect:

  • Stock Ticker Symbols: When you hover over stock tickers (e.g., $AAPL, $TSLA) on supported websites (Twitter/X.com, Reddit), the Extension detects these symbols to provide financial data.
  • Extension Usage Data: Basic operational data such as whether the Extension is enabled/disabled and your preference settings (stored locally).
  • Technical Information: Anonymous technical data for debugging purposes, including error logs and performance metrics.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of displaying Max Pain data for detected stock tickers
  • Improve the Extension's performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Maintain and enhance user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All Extension settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

3.2 Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with:

  • Yahoo Finance API: To fetch real-time stock options data (using your residential IP address)
  • Cloudflare Workers: For processing financial calculations (Max Pain algorithm)

These services may receive stock ticker symbols but do not receive any personal identifying information.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • Secure data processing in isolated environments
  • Regular security assessments

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share anonymous, aggregated data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our Extension (e.g., Cloudflare for hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You can:

  • Enable/disable the Extension at any time
  • Clear local storage data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove all locally stored data

5.2 Do Not Track

Our Extension does not respond to Do Not Track signals as we do not track users across websites.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. International Data Transfers

As a Chrome extension, data processing occurs on your local device. Any API calls to third-party services may involve international data transfers, but these transfers only involve stock ticker symbols, not personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly.
